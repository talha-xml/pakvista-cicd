Hunza Valley

Hunza Valley is one of the most beautiful tourist destinations in Pakistan. Located in Gilgit-Baltistan, it is famous for its mountains, lakes, pleasant weather, and breathtaking landscapes.

Popular Attractions

  • Attabad Lake
  • Baltit Fort
  • Altit Fort
  • Passu Cones
  • Khunjerab Pass
